About Me

My name is Lukaz. I'm a mix between a musician, an activist, an artist, and a programmer. Originally from a small town in Louisiana, I moved to California a few years ago to follow my passion for tech. I began working with tech & communtiy focused non-profits in Oakland and quickly realized the need for me to improve my programming skills and familiarity with the Bay's tech industry. I have always loved solving problems and and seeing how tech changes the world around us. I have years of customer support and management experiences that I can bring to the table, as well as my skills with web development. My willingness to learn and thirst for knowledge spurs me on to keep learning more and more about current and emerging tech. Recently I attended a full-stack web immersive program at General Assembly in San Francisco. Here I built full-stack web applications, had the opportunity to learn new skills, hone and improve others, and work within a team. I'm always excited to take on a project and can't wait to see what the future holds for myself and for the tech industry as a whole!
